Gokula-Gurukula

Traditional educational institution following the time-tested Gurukula system of learning

Philosophy and Approach

Our Gokula-Gurukula follows the ancient Indian tradition of holistic education where students live with their teacher (Guru) in a residential setting. This system emphasizes:

  • Character development alongside academic excellence
  • Direct teacher-student relationship fostering personal growth
  • Integration of practical life skills with theoretical knowledge
  • Emphasis on moral and spiritual values
  • Connection with nature and sustainable living practices
  • Preservation of traditional arts, crafts, and knowledge systems

About Gomathe Devalaya and Gokula-Gurukula

Sacred temple complex integrated with our educational institution

The Sacred Complex

Gomathe Devalaya serves as the spiritual heart of our institution, where the divine presence guides our educational and service activities. The temple is dedicated to Gomatha (the sacred cow) and represents the harmonious integration of spiritual and educational pursuits.

Features of the Complex:

  • Main temple dedicated to Gomatha with daily ritual worship
  • Educational halls and residential facilities for students
  • Library containing ancient scriptures and modern educational materials
  • Meditation halls for spiritual practice and contemplation
  • Organic garden and cow shelter within the premises
  • Cultural performance spaces for traditional arts
